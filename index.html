Bio

After my studies at the UNT College of Music, I worked as a freelance jazz musician in the Dallas-Fort Worth area and taught private guitar lessons to over 40 students a week for about 3 years.

From 2017 to 2018 I worked as a resident guitarist for luxury cruise lines Regent and Oceania, where my primary duty was to sight-read music (performing new sheet music daily with little or no practice/rehearsal).

I began studying coding from simple personal interest. Now I am studying web development at DigitalCrafts's Houston campus to pursue a career in programming.

My current project, Music Tools, is a Python 3 module that uses object-oriented programming to represent concepts and structures found in music theory so that a musician-programmer, such as myself, can dynamically create common patterns or calculations of musical concepts.
